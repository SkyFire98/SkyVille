Good Quotes

A page for motivation, wisdom, and more, from people good and bad, popular and unknown.

  • The best way to predict the future is to create it. ~ Peter Drucker

  • You'll live. Only the best get killed ~ Charles de Gaulle

  • I can resist everything except temptation ~ Oscar Wilde

  • Never lie in bed at night asking yourself questions you can't answer. ~ Charles M. Schulz

  • If no one knows when a person is going to die, how can we say he died prematurely? ~ George Carlin

  • A word to the wise ain't necessary - it's the stupid ones that need the advice. ~ Bill Cosby

  • Things do not happen; Things are made to happen. ~ John F. Kennedy

  • Your talent is God's gift to you. What you do with it is your gift back to God. ~ Leo Buscaglia

  • We can't help everyone, but everyone can help someone. ~ Ronald Reagan

  • If opportunity doesn't knock, build a door. ~ Milton Berle

  • A woman would never make a nuclear bomb. They would never make a weapon that kills - no, no. They'd make a weapon that makes you feel bad for a while. ~ Robin Williams

  • The only true wisdom is in knowing you know nothing. ~ Socrates

  • Don't taunt the alligator until you've crossed the creek. ~ Dan Rather

  • Some folks are wise and some are otherwise. ~ Tobias Smollett

  • Nine-tenths of wisdom is being wise in time. ~ Theodore Roosevelt

  • Two things are infinite: the universe and human stupidity; and I'm not sure about the universe. ~ Albert Einstein

  • Always forgive your enemies; nothing annoys them so much. ~ Oscar Wilde

  • Obstacles do not exist to be surrendered to, but only to be broken. ~ Adolph Hitler

  • There is a wisdom of the head, and a wisdom of the heart. ~ Charles Dickens

  • A successful man is one who can lay a firm foundation with the bricks others have thrown at him ~ David Brinkley

  • Reflections in the heart are more pronounced than those we view in a mirror ~ Dixie Waters

  • Every man is a fool for at least five minutes every day; wisdom consists in not exceeding the limit ~ Elbert Hubbard

  • The fool doth think he is wise, but the wise man knows himself to be a fool ~ William Shakespere, As You Like It

  • Most of the shadows of this life are caused by our standing in our own sunshine ~ Ralph Waldo Emerson

  • A life is like a garden. Perfect moments can be had, but not preserved, except in memory. Live Long And Prosper. ~ Leonard Nimoy
